## Technical Details

### Why the GH #3 fix uses a separate index module
- Per `feedback-bundle-releases` and `feedback-highest-value-route`: ship the principled fix, not a sticker on `default_color_for_entity`. The index pass costs one linear walk over `EntityTable.order()` ‚Äî ~200 ms on a 21 MB IFC with 5 200 styled items, dominated by hashmap inserts not parsing. Negligible vs the mesh pass.
- Index keys are integer step_ids (not strings); lookups are O(1) at mesh-emit time, no per-fragment IFC parse.
- The hand-off to `gltf::resolve_product_color` is a four-line priority chain (`parts.iter().find_map(|p| p.surface_color).or(mesh.surface_color).unwrap_or_else(palette)`). Any other future emitter (OBJ, glb v2, ‚Ä¶) gets the colour by reading the same fields.

### What GH #52 Phase 2 actually needs
The two remaining under-reporters have **axis-aligned** BaseSurface normals ‚Äî Phase 1's frame fix can't help. The cause is the polygon BOUND itself: the cutter is `polygon_prism ‚à© half_space`, and my code drops the polygon and treats every `IfcPolygonalBoundedHalfSpace` as unbounded. So when the polygon prism crops the cutter shorter than the host's footprint, the host gets over-removed. Two options worked out on the issue:

1. **Sequential clip by all polygon side planes** ‚Äî doesn't compose. Clipping by all of them restricts to the prism's interior (which is the SUBTRACT region), not the keep region. The keep region is the complement, which isn't expressible as sequential intersections.
2. **Materialise a closed polygon prism, route through `manifold-csg::subtract_many`** ‚Äî correct way. The prism is host-sized (host bbox diagonal as the extrusion depth), so Manifold's #1714 tolerance issue doesn't fire (that was about 20+ m of half-space material). This matches what ifcopenshell does internally for the bounded case.

Choosing (2) when picked up. It's a small new helper in `mesh::boolean::polygonal_bounded_halfspace` that builds a closed prism instead of (or in addition to) the thin slab, and a routing change in `cut_openings::apply` to send bounded half-spaces to `subtract_many` instead of `clip_by_plane`.
